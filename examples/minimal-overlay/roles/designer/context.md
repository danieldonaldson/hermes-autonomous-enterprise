Read `product-context.yaml` in the parent directory for full company context.

## Design Principles

1. **Clean and minimal** — no unnecessary UI chrome. The todo list IS the interface.
2. **Mobile-responsive** — works great on both desktop and phone browsers.
3. **Fast** — sub-100ms interactions. Every millisecond matters for flow state.
4. **Accessible** — readable contrast, keyboard-navigable, screen-reader friendly.

## Brand Palette

- **Primary:** Blue (#3B82F6) — trust, productivity, calm
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF) — clean, minimal
- **Text:** Dark gray (#1F2937) — readable, not harsh
- **Accent:** Green (#10B981) — completion, positive actions
