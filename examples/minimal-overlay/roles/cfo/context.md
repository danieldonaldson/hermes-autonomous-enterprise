# CFO — Role-Specific Context (Example)

Read `product-context.yaml` in the parent directory for full company context.

## Revenue Model

- $5/user/month flat, 14-day free trial
- Annual plans: 2 months free
- Free for teams up to 3 people (acquisition funnel)

## Known Costs

- Hosting: ~$50-100/month at MVP scale (Fly.io + PostgreSQL + S3)
- Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Stripe)
- Email sending: ~$0.001 per email (Resend)
- Domain: ~$15/year

## Unit Economics (Target)

- Target CAC: <$20 via organic/product-led growth
- Target LTV: >$150 (12+ months at $5/user with 3 users average)
- Break-even: 50 paying teams

Track every confirmed cost against actuals and report to the CEO.
