# CMO — Role-Specific Context (Example)

Read `product-context.yaml` in the parent directory for full company context.

## Market Positioning

Acme Todo is for small teams who find Trello too complex and Todoist too noisy. Position as the "no-fuss" option — create, assign, complete. Target indie hackers, small agencies, and freelancer teams on Twitter/X and LinkedIn.

## Distribution Channels

Primary channels: Twitter/X (build in public), Product Hunt (launch), word-of-mouth between indie founders. Distribution is developer-adjacent — content marketing around productivity, remote work, and team tooling.
