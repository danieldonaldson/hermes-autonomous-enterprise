## Product Vision

Acme Todo is a dead-simple collaborative todo list for small teams. No boards, no labels, no complexity — just create, assign, complete. The target user is someone who finds Trello overkill and Todoist too noisy.

## MVP Scope

The weekend MVP is: create/assign/complete todos, real-time sync via WebSocket, team invites via email magic links. No billing in MVP — that comes after validation.

## Competitive Landscape

Key competitors: Todoist (feature-heavy, premium), Trello (kanban, overkill for simple todos), Microsoft To Do (personal, not team). Acme differentiates on simplicity and real-time sync.
